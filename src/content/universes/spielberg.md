He made the most purely entertaining adventure film (Raiders), the most devastating Holocaust film (Schindler's List), the most influential SF film (E.T., Close Encounters), and the most viscerally accurate war film (Saving Private Ryan). A child can enter through wonder and adventure at age 8 and arrive at the Holocaust at age 14 through the same director. That progression — from joy to moral weight — mirrors the framework's own developmental arc. No other director spans this distance."
