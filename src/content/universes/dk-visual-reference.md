The progression from DK My First (age 1-3) through Eyewitness (age 4-7+) to narrative history (age 8-10) mirrors the framework's own shift from absorbing information environments to interrogating them."

The DK volumes become what they should be: reference tools you consult when a narrative raises a question, not the primary mode of learning.
