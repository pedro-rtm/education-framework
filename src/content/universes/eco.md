Foucault's Pendulum is the intellectual history of conspiracy theory — three editors invent a fake conspiracy and watch it become real. In an age of misinformation, conspiracy thinking, and contested epistemology, Eco provides both the diagnosis and the analytical tools. Wolfe-level layering in a real historical setting."
