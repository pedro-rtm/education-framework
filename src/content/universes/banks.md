In a world where AI automation, UBI, and abundance economics are live questions, Banks already explored the end state: what do humans do when they don't need to work, when resources are unlimited, when AI Minds are vastly more intelligent than humans? The answers — hedonism, interference in less advanced civilisations, existential games, art — are not reassuring. The framework has economics, entrepreneurship, and technology universes that assume scarcity. Banks asks: what happens when scarcity ends?"
