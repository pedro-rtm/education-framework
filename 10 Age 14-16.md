## Developmental Reality

Adult cognitive capacity is essentially present. The framework's earlier investments either compound visibly here or their absence becomes apparent. The gap is visible and will widen. What's still developing: emotional regulation under social pressure and impulse control in high-stakes peer contexts.

## Activity Map

| [[02 The Ten Targets\|Target]] | Core Approach | Products & Resources |
|---|---|---|
| **Self-Efficacy** | Real-world projects with real-world stakes. Not school assignments — actual things that can succeed or fail in the world. | [[#Resources\|Regeneron Science Talent Search or Google Science Fair]] · Open-source contribution or deployed app with real users · [[#Resources\|Startup or Project Pitch Competition]] |
| **Self-Compassion** | Stoic texts as operational tools, not curriculum. Argument: these are the tools the most effective people in history used to manage the psychological pressure you're feeling right now. | [[#Resources\|Meditations (continue daily)]] · [[#Resources\|Letters from a Stoic — Seneca]] · [[#Resources\|Enchiridion — Epictetus]] |
| **Agency** | Real decisions with real consequences — financial, academic, social. You are a consultant, not a manager. | Personal budget for all personal expenses (clothing, social, equipment). Full ownership. Natural consequences without rescue. |
| **Grit** | Competitive pursuit where failure is public and the trajectory is long. No rotation out when it gets hard. | Competitive athletics season with training camp. Go: Kyu to Dan transition — the hardest plateau in the game. Warhammer 40K campaign-level play and tournaments. |
| **First-Principles Reasoning** | Primary sources replace textbooks. Derive everything. Argue with experts. | [[#Resources\|The Feynman Lectures on Physics Vol 1]] · [[#Resources\|Spivak Calculus]] · Popper's Logic of Scientific Discovery (key argument) |
| **Obsessive Focus** | The obsession now produces output for an external audience. Deep work protocol implemented formally. | [[#Resources\|Deep Work — Cal Newport]] — implement: 90–120 min blocks, no devices, measure output not time. "What would you build if you had 6 months and no school obligations?" Then build it within constraints. |
| **Ambiguity Tolerance** | Genuine open questions. Primary sources where answers aren't settled. | [[#Resources\|Thinking Fast and Slow — Daniel Kahneman]] · [[#Resources\|The Black Swan — Nassim Taleb]] · [[#Resources\|Antifragile — Nassim Taleb]] · [[#Resources\|The Structure of Scientific Revolutions — Thomas Kuhn]] (full text now) |
| **Genuine Curiosity** | Autonomous inquiry. They identify questions; they design investigations. You resource. | [[#Resources\|Research Mentorship with Working Professional]] — one senior practitioner in their area of interest, monthly. |
| **Verbal Intelligence** | Real audiences, real stakes. Lead, don't just participate. | [[#Resources\|Improv Comedy Classes with Performance Nights]] · [[#Resources\|Startup or Project Pitch Competition]] · Teaching: teach something they know well to someone who knows less. |
| **Physical Hardship Tolerance** | Adult-adjacent challenge. Genuinely difficult. | [[#Resources\|Wim Hof Method Full Protocol]] — properly supervised. Multi-day wilderness expedition with navigation and self-sufficiency. Combat sports competition with weight management. |
