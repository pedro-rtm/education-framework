## Developmental Reality

Abstract reasoning is emerging. Peer comparison begins — "am I good at this?" is now a live question, making self-efficacy and self-compassion work urgent. This is the first window where deliberate virtue cultivation produces real results. Executive function is actively being built through play — the most important window for protecting genuine curiosity before school systematically redirects it.

## [[03 Mental Models Progression\|Mental Models]] at This Stage

See [[03 Mental Models Progression#Ages 4–7 Early Causal and Social Models|Ages 4–7 section]].

Key installations: **Inversion** via [[#Resources\|Calvin and Hobbes Complete Collection Fantagraphics]] and Avatar ("what if the villain was right?") · **Incentives** ("why does that character want that?" as a reflex after any story) · **Systems/Interdependence** via field trips.

**The dinner table ritual:** Pick one character. "What does [character] actually want? Why? Is that reasonable? What would you do?" 10 minutes, every few days.
