## Developmental Reality

The goal is transfer: everything built over seventeen years becomes their internal operating system. Your role shifts fundamentally — from architect to consultant. They design their own life. You are available to think with, not to manage.

## What Success Looks Like

1. They ask "why did they make that decision?" when encountering any made thing
2. They update beliefs in response to evidence without ego catastrophe
3. They attempt hard things and recover from failure with analytical curiosity rather than shame
4. They sit with "I don't know" long enough to reason toward an answer rather than pattern-match to a comfortable one
5. They find things genuinely interesting and follow that interest wherever it goes
6. They know what their body can endure
7. They can think clearly out loud, under pressure, for an audience that disagrees

None of these can be directly installed. All of them can be made more likely.
